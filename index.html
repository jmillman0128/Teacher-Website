Hello there!

I'm Mr. Millman, educator and proud operator of the "Star Mill."

Stars are among the most powerful forces in the universe, radiating energy, warmth, and ultimately making life possible. I believe my students are much the same: each one a star with the potential to shine in their own unique way.

The "Star Mill" is what I call my classroom: a place where students develop their strengths, discover their potential, and grow into the brightest versions of themselves.

"We are made of star-stuff. We are a way for the universe to know itself." — Carl Sagan

In this class, we work to understand not just the world around us, but the potential within us.
